What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on your height and weight. It is used to categorize individuals into different weight categories, such as underweight, normal weight, overweight, and obese.

  • Simple calculation based on height and weight.
  • Provides an estimate of body fat percentage.
  • Used to assess health risks associated with weight.
